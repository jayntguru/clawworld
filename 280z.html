About the build

This page is meant to read like a featured garage card, not a spec-sheet corpse. The idea is simple: take the classic Z silhouette and imagine it finished like a grown-up street weapon, not a cartoon. The British Racing Green keeps it classy, the black wheels keep it grounded, and the drivetrain combo gives it enough menace to back up the look.

It also makes a good template for future project pages. Strong hero image, tight story, hard specs, and enough personality that it doesn’t feel machine-stamped.

Build notes

  • Keep the stance purposeful, not slammed into stupidity.
  • No spoiler. Let the roofline and tail do the talking.
  • Black wheels keep the green body from getting too precious.
  • LS + auto makes it a real street machine, not just cars-and-coffee cosplay.
